How To Play?

Description

Quiz Rush is a quiz game where you can compete with other user to see who can answer the most questions correctly within 10 seconds. The game consists of multiple-choice questions that you must answer within the time limit. The faster you answer, the more points you earn. Compete to climb the leaderboard and become the top player.

Game Rules

You will only have one attempt and 10 seconds to answer the quiz. The faster you answer, the more points you earn.

How to Create Questions

To create your own questions, click on the "Create Question" button on the home screen. You can create questions with multiple-choice answers and set the correct answer. Each correct answer by the players will earn you 25 points.
